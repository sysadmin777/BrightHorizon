Bright Horizons

Problem: College students and job seekers have uncertain, and often complex, paths to fulfill career objectives. Because their goals seem so far away, they often lack the drive and organization required to complete their short-term tasks.

Solution: An application that provides a mechanism to develop roadmaps and rewards as incentive for accomplishing objectives. The application allows learner and accreditor roles, each has their own functions. The objective of the app is to develop tasks and complete them for points that can be redeemed for prizes, such as certification vouchers and educational benefits.
